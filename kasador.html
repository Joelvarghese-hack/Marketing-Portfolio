Overview

The Brief

Kasador is an emerging indie rock band from Kingston, Ontario. Under the Nova Solutions student agency, I developed a full digital marketing plan to help them convert rapid TikTok momentum into consistent engagement on Spotify, Instagram, and at live shows.

The core problem: strong top-of-funnel awareness on TikTok but weak conversion infrastructure. Fans discovered the band but didn't follow, stream, or show up.

Research

Audience Persona

Challenges

What We Were Up Against

Low ROI on Meta Ads

Previous Meta ad campaigns were not converting — audiences were too broad and creative wasn't tailored to where fans were in the funnel.

No Retargeting Infrastructure

TikTok viewers were not being retargeted on Meta or via email. There was no pixel, no list, and no mechanism to re-engage warm audiences after initial discovery.

Weak Cross-Platform Conversion

High TikTok visibility was not translating to Spotify streams or Instagram follows. Each platform felt isolated with no unified fan journey connecting them.

Fans Missing Show Announcements

No real-time communication channel meant fans weren't hearing about live shows until too late. No email list, no push notifications, no geo-targeted reminders.

Strategy

Four-Tactic Plan

Each tactic was chosen to address a specific conversion gap, moving fans from TikTok discovery to live show attendance.

01

TikTok Retargeting via Meta Ads

Custom audiences built from TikTok viewers, retargeted with concert footage and behind-the-scenes content on Instagram and Facebook to drive Spotify streams and follows.

02

Segmented Email via Mailchimp

Geo-targeted show reminders and time-sensitive ticket drops for fans near tour dates. Separate flows for first-time subscribers vs returning fans.

03

Local Business Collaborations

Activated the North Roast Coffee partnership and expanded to cafes and indie stores along the tour route. In-venue promotion converting coffee regulars into concert-goers.

04

SEO Optimisation

Fixed missing meta descriptions, improved page speed, added keyword-rich blog content for tour dates and releases, and improved image alt text for better search discovery.
